Home made food

All our dishes and tapas are based on traditional recipes from all over the country, prepared by our Spanish chefs with seasonal produce.

Great service

Our staff members can give you advice and offer recommendations about all our food and what wine or beer goes best with your order.

Vegetarian and vegan options

We have a wide range of vegan and vegetarian dishes, tapas and desserts. Check our menu or ask our staff for allergies and intolerances.

We are on the road!

Look out for our food truck at festivals, food markets and other events to try our tapas on the go. Check our social media for updates or where to find us.

Catering service

We also offer a catering service for weddings, birthday parties and any type of event that you are planning. Email or call us for more information.
